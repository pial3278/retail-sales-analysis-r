# Retail Sales Analysis using R

## Overview
This project analyzes a 2023 retail sales dataset to evaluate overall performance, customer demographics, product category trends, pricing strategy, and seasonality. The objective is to generate data-driven business insights and actionable recommendations.

## Business Questions
- What is the overall sales performance?
- Which product categories drive transactions vs. revenue?
- How do customer demographics influence spending behavior?
- Are there seasonal revenue patterns?
- Do older customers spend more per transaction?

## Key Insights
- Total revenue of **$456,000** generated from **1,000 transactions** with an average transaction value of **$456**.
- **Clothing** drove the highest transaction volume (35.1%), while **Electronics** generated the highest revenue ($156,905).
- **Gen Z customers** had the highest average transaction value ($491), outperforming older age groups.
- **May** was the strongest revenue month ($53,150), with strong performance also observed in Q4.

## Recommendations
- Use Clothing as a customer acquisition category while prioritizing Electronics for profitability.
- Target Gen Z customers with premium bundles and digital-first marketing.
- Increase marketing and inventory focus during high-revenue months, especially May and Q4.
